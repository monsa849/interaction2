Hold These Hands
Take Me As I am

My turn

on the bed
where green grass lies
we sit holding hands you and I
A flower in bloom
Hanging down from the sky
I love you so much it hurts
just let me come inside

filthy games we like to play
where devils lay their thorns on the ground

a beat of hop
on city streets
summer runs and boys play drums.
sparklers bounce
as we lay here now
two words unspoken.
